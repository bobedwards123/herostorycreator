
        This story is set in Sydney in 1953. John is the protagonist, he is a     Photojournalist that has a dream of to fly but is scared of the dark. 
        
        One day, a house burning down happens which changes everything. John's fears of the dark are amplified in having to deal with losing important possessions. This causes the protagonist great dispair.
        sorrowed by the event, John is called into the unknown. Suddenly the comforts of normalcy begin to fade and our protagonist is lost. 

        
        John soon discovers that things have changed. The world is no longer as it seemed, the protagonist meets Darcy who is a     Graphic designer. Darcy is different from John in that he is not scared of the unknown.

        The world that John has entered is not the same as his place of comfort. Things are different here that that which he knew back home. 

        
        As John goes deeper and deeper into this new place, John faces greater and greater challenges. his skills at navigating the new world are limited. he must work with his new friend Darcy to develop them.
        he must continue to face internal and external challenges, slowing approaching the ultimate challenge. 

        
        Finally John faces the ultimate challenge, confronting Seth who wants to     Penciller. to be a great person's skills are much stronger than Seth's but John has developed them through training. 


        
        The challenge is incredibly difficult and causes John to complely doubt himself. In the end it is almost over but he gets the strength to continue and ultimately succeeds against Seth


        
        Finally John is able to return home. Having achieved success at overcoming the ultimate challenge, brining home the lessons to his family and friends
        and sharing his gifts with the community.
