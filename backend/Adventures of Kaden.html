Adventures of Kaden in Vanuatu

This story is set in Vanuatu in 1991. Kaden is the protagonist, he is a Set dresser that has a dream of to be a great person but is scared of the public.

One day, Kaden is let go which changes everything. Kaden's fears of the public are amplified in having to deal with the financial pressure of having to regain funds to pay for bills and everyday living. This causes the protagonist great sorrow. angered by the event, Kaden is called into the unknown. Suddenly the comforts of normalcy begin to fade and our protagonist is lost.

Kaden soon discovers that things have changed. The world is no longer as it seemed, the protagonist meets Julian who is a Event planner. Julian is different from Kaden in that he is not scared of the unknown. The world that Kaden has entered is not the same as his place of comfort. Things are different here that that which he knew back home.

As Kaden goes deeper and deeper into this new place, Kaden faces greater and greater challenges. his skills at navigating the new world are limited. he must work with his new friend Julian to develop them. he must continue to face internal and external challenges, slowing approaching the ultimate challenge.

Along this journey Kaden undergoes a number of character developemnts through the progression of the subplots which include overcoming inner psychological tourments and overcoming inner psychological tourments. These test his ability to understand people and conflict, ensuring character developent in the process. Finally Kaden faces the ultimate challenge, confronting Collin who wants to Dancer. to be a great person's skills are much stronger than Collin's but Kaden has developed them through training.

The challenge is incredibly difficult and causes Kaden to complely doubt himself. In the end it is almost over but he gets the strength to continue and ultimately succeeds against Collin

Finally Kaden is able to return home. Having achieved success at overcoming the ultimate challenge, brining home the lessons to his family and friends and sharing his gifts with the community.
