
        This story is set in Sydney in 1965. Seth is the protagonist, he is a lawyer that has a dream of to fly but is scared of going outside. 
        
        One day, a Car crash happens which changes everything. Seth's fears of going outside are amplified in having to deal with losing loved ones. This causes the protagonist great dismay.
        hatreded by the event, Seth is called into the unknown. Suddenly the comforts of normalcy begin to fade and our protagonist is lost. 

        
        Seth soon discovers that things have changed. The world is no longer as it seemed, the protagonist meets John who is a accountant. John is different from Seth in that he is not scared of the unknown.

        The world that Seth has entered is not the same as his place of comfort. Things are different here that that which he knew back home. 

        
        As Seth goes deeper and deeper into this new place, Seth faces greater and greater challenges. his skills at navigating the new world are limited. he must work with his new friend John to develop them.
        he must continue to face internal and external challenges, slowing approaching the ultimate challenge. 

        
        Finally Seth faces the ultimate challenge, confronting John who wants to waiter. to be a rockstar's skills are much stronger than John's but Seth has developed them through training. 


        
        The challenge is incredibly difficult and causes Seth to complely doubt himself. In the end it is almost over but he gets the strength to continue and ultimately succeeds against John


        
        Finally Seth is able to return home. Having achieved success at overcoming the ultimate challenge, brining home the lessons to his family and friends
