Adventures of Andres in Canada

This story is set in Canada in 1920. Andres is the protagonist, he is a Animator that has a dream of to be a rockstar but is scared of getting old.

One day, Andres loses the love of their life which changes everything. Andres's fears of getting old are amplified in having to deal with the emotional challanges of rebuilding trust and confidence. This causes the protagonist great hatred. dismayed by the event, Andres is called into the unknown. Suddenly the comforts of normalcy begin to fade and our protagonist is lost.

Andres soon discovers that things have changed. The world is no longer as it seemed, the protagonist meets Ryan who is a Illustrator. Ryan is different from Andres in that he is not scared of the unknown. The world that Andres has entered is not the same as his place of comfort. Things are different here that that which he knew back home.

As Andres goes deeper and deeper into this new place, Andres faces greater and greater challenges. his skills at navigating the new world are limited. he must work with his new friend Ryan to develop them. he must continue to face internal and external challenges, slowing approaching the ultimate challenge.

Along this journey Andres undergoes a number of character developemnts through the progression of the subplots which include figuring out a difficult problem and building a strong relationship with a friend. These test his ability to understand people and conflict, ensuring character developent in the process. Finally Andres faces the ultimate challenge, confronting Evan who wants to Photojournalist. to fly's skills are much stronger than Evan's but Andres has developed them through training.

The challenge is incredibly difficult and causes Andres to complely doubt himself. In the end it is almost over but he gets the strength to continue and ultimately succeeds against Evan

Finally Andres is able to return home. Having achieved success at overcoming the ultimate challenge, brining home the lessons to his family and friends and sharing his gifts with the community.
