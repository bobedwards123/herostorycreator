
        This story is set in Paris in 2033. John is the protagonist, he is a lawyer that has a dream of to fly but is scared of the unknown. 
        
        One day, a house burning down happens which changes everything. John's fears of the unknown are amplified in having to deal with losing important possessions. This causes the protagonist great dispair.
        dismayed by the event, John is called into the unknown. Suddenly the comforts of normalcy begin to fade and our protagonist is lost. 

        
        John soon discovers that things have changed. The world is no longer as it seemed, the protagonist meets George who is a waiter. George is different from John in that he is not scared of the unknown.

        The world that John has entered is not the same as his place of comfort. Things are different here that that which he knew back home. 

        
        As John goes deeper and deeper into this new place, John faces greater and greater challenges. his skills at navigating the new world are limited. he must work with his new friend George to develop them.
        he must continue to face internal and external challenges, slowing approaching the ultimate challenge. 

        
        Finally John faces the ultimate challenge, confronting Darcy who wants to lawyer. to be a rockstar's skills are much stronger than Darcy's but John has developed them through training. 


        
        The challenge is incredibly difficult and causes John to complely doubt himself. In the end it is almost over but he gets the strength to continue and ultimately succeeds against Darcy


        
        Finally John is able to return home. Having achieved success at overcoming the ultimate challenge, brining home the lessons to his family and friends
        and sharing his gifts with the community.
